We are a startup specializing in Self Sustainable Modern Gaushalas, incorporating hydroponic crop growing, terrace farming, and polyhouse/net house construction. With over 50 successful projects nationwide, we offer services to existing gaushalas, commercialize milk production, and prioritize the health, fitness, and productivity of cattle. Our expertise includes hydroponic crop cultivation, polyhouse construction, and comprehensive cattle care.

Mission

By 2026, our mission is to establish 40 cow Self Sustainable Modern Gaushalas in each Gram Panchayat across Uttar Pradesh. Our vision encompasses the implementation of high-tech milk production and distribution systems in major cities, as well as enhancing land quality by 70-80 percent in the surrounding areas for organic crop cultivation. Additionally, we aim to create significant employment opportunities in the rural sector through the development of our Self Sustainable Modern Gaushalas.

Vision for Self Sustainable Modern Gaushala 

Our vision for a Self Sustainable Modern Gaushala is centered around providing the public with hygienic milk at an affordable price, commercializing milk production in our country, and developing high-quality dairy products that contribute to people's well-being. We prioritize the health and well-being of cattle, recognizing them as a valuable national resource. This involves creating a proper environment, offering a nutritious diet, and providing necessary medical facilities to ensure the production of top-quality milk. Furthermore, we aim to maximize the utilization of cattle resources, including dung, urine, and energy, for both the profitability and growth of the cattle and the gaushala itself. Our dedication lies in promoting a healthy, prosperous, and clean society. We strive to enhance soil fertility by utilizing cattle waste to create organic fertilizers and produce organic crops that benefit the people. This concept of a Self Sustainable Modern Gaushala encapsulates our commitment to sustainability, productivity, and the overall well-being of both humans and animals.
